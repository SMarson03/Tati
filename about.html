When expression remains integral to its roots, Mind Integrity is achieved.

Tatianna Anderson

Growing up in large family in Southern Westchester with strong spiritual and religious roots, has been an intergal part of her artistic development.Painting has given her an outlet from the harsh realities of life. :) ;) :P

CEO & Founder

Majority of my art work has evolving meanings, stemming from dreams and visions that come to me. I use my imagination and materials to illustrate what I’ve seen and experienced. Think of the canvas as a “room” and as you engage with my work, a conversation is being had in this space I’ve created. Sometimes they can be interpreted premonitions of the future. Many of times I look back at them in shock, life occurs as it does; and here my painting is “speaking sound sentiments”. I’ve started viewing painting as 50% intention and the meaning will come later, but subconsciously I will always know.
